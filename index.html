A Brief Biography...

Upon graduating from the University of California, Irvine, I became a technical recruiter which formally introduced me to the world of programming and its amazing industry. I was constantly talking to programmers, managers and CTOs about the hottest and latest technologies but had no idea at the time as to why it got them so excited. I became curious. Inspired. Motivated.

I decided I wanted to be on the other side of my recruiting career - as a candidate. I enrolled in a web development program and immediately became intrigued. Programming allowed me to combine my knack for studying with my affinity for problem solving. Every day brought on a new challenge. But with each challenge I gained an opportunity to learn and improve. I was able to finally understand the excitement of my former candidates. However, this is just the start of my journey. I can't wait to see the future of software and programming and how I can affect it.
